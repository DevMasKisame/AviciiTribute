Childhood Journey

Tim Bergling was born in Stockholm 1989 September 8 .He is the son of Kals Bergling and Ankin Liden . He is a product of a office supply mananger and actress. Has three siblings Linda Sterner , David berling and Anton Korberg . He began mixing at the age of eight . He was inspired by his brother who was also a Dj .Taught himself to use FL Studio that was pirated . He started making his own music at age 16. Two years later Tim got himself a deal at Dejfitts plays label. He was a member of the Laidback Luke Forums and demonstrated his deep house music sauce. By 2010 Tim was an extreme skilled producer and was rapidly releasing music .He then released his hit song "Seek Bromance" that reached the top 20 in several countries including France and the UK(United Kingdom).
